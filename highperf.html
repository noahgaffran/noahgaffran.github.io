High Performance Rocketry

If it's not going high and fast, what's the point?

Here are a few high performance rockets I've worked on that flew on commercial motors.

J425 minimum diameter rocket

This was my first solo attempt at a high performance rocket. It was 100% scratch built, with custom rolled fibreglass tubes, custom nosecone, carbon fibre skinned fins, and a 3d printed avionics bay. The recovery used the single bay system pioneered by the AeroPac 100k project and also used by Kip Daugirdas on his Project Mesos space shot.

This rocket flew out of a custom tower (electrical conduit tubes and 3d printed end caps) at URRG in New York state in April 2024. The apogee was 9,437 ft AGL, and the maximum velocity was Mach 1.9. The rocket was successfully recovered on a nearby field with no damage, the onboard GPS working perfectly.
