About me

Hi! My name is Noah Gaffran, and welcome to my website! I'm a mechanical engineering graduate, an outdoors enthusiast, a low talent jazz musician, and an amateur rocketeer.

2018

Graduated high school, moved to Ontario, started engineering at Queen's, joined QRET.

2020

Got my first research assistant job in the Materials Engineering dept at Queen's. Worked on my first paper that got published!

2021

Started my internship year, worked for four months as a project coordinator for a mechanical construction company, then changed jobs to an engineering assistant at a hydropower consultancy. Joined the UBC Rocket team for the year.

2022

Drove across the country from BC to Ontario, attended my first IREC and Launch Canada.

2023

Got high power rocketry certified, won Launch Canada as my final act as an undergrad, worked as a research assistant on detonation waves, started a masters in composites.

2024

Volunteered at Launch Canada for the first time, did a whole ton of rocketry, over 50,000 ft total in the year.

2025

Built a liquid rocket, moved back to BC.
